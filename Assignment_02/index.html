The Serenity Resort

Where nature meets comfort
If you are looking for a peaceful getaway in the lap of nature, The Serenity Resort in Coorg is the perfect choice for you.
This resort is located on a hilltop, surrounded by lush greenery and breathtaking views of the Western Ghats. You can enjoy the fresh mountain air, the soothing sound of birds, and the aroma of coffee plantations.
The resort offers spacious and comfortable rooms, equipped with modern amenities and elegant decor.
You can also indulge in various activities, such as trekking, cycling, plantation tours, and yoga. The resort has a multi-cuisine restaurant, a coffee lounge, a spa, and a swimming pool.
Whether you want to relax, rejuvenate, or explore, The Serenity Resort will make your stay memorable. Book your room today and experience the magic of Coorg
